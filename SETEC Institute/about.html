Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, accessible, and affordable education in the fields of Information Technology, Design, and Business. We aim to equip our students with the critical thinking, technical skills, and ethical values needed to excel in their careers and contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Cambodia and the wider ASEAN region, recognized for our innovative teaching methods, strong industry partnerships, and the outstanding success of our graduates in the global workforce.

Our History & Milestones

2008

The Beginning

SETEC Institute was founded with a focus on providing specialized IT training to meet the growing demands of the digital economy in Cambodia.

2012

Expansion of Programs

Introduced new faculties for Graphic Design and Business Administration, broadening our academic offerings to a wider range of students.

2016

Official Accreditation

Received full accreditation from the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport, cementing our status as a trusted higher education institution.

2022

New Campus Inauguration

Opened a new state-of-the-art campus equipped with modern labs, a digital library, and collaborative learning spaces to enhance the student experience.
