Our Academic Programs

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Our flagship IT program provides a comprehensive education in software engineering, database management, network security, and web development. Students gain hands-on experience in our state-of-the-art computer labs.

Core Subjects:

  • Object-Oriented Programming
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Network Administration
  • Cybersecurity Fundamentals

Bachelor of Arts in Graphic Design

This program fosters creativity and technical skill. Students learn design principles, typography, branding, and digital illustration using industry-standard software like Adobe Creative Suite.

Core Subjects:

  • Design Theory & History
  • Digital Photography
  • User Interface (UI) Design
  • Branding & Identity

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The BBA program prepares students for leadership roles. The curriculum covers marketing, finance, human resources, and entrepreneurship, with a focus on real-world case studies.

Core Subjects:

  • Principles of Marketing
  • Corporate Finance
  • International Business
  • Strategic Management
