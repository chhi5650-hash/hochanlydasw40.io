How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to become a part of our community.

1

Choose Your Program

Explore our wide range of academic programs and find the one that aligns with your career goals.

2

Check Requirements

Review the entry requirements for your chosen program to ensure you are eligible for admission.

3

Prepare Documents

Gather all necessary documents, including your high school diploma, national ID card, and photos.

4

Submit Application

Complete the application form online or visit our campus to submit your documents in person.

Entry Requirements

For Bachelor's Degree Programs

  • High School Diploma (or equivalent)
  • Passing score on the National High School Examination
  • Copy of National ID Card or Passport
  • 4x6 photographs (4 copies)

For Associate's Degree Programs

  • High School Diploma (or equivalent)
  • Copy of National ID Card or Passport
  • 4x6 photographs (4 copies)
  • No entrance exam required
